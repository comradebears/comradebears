First, let's talk about the art.

Our art [mostly] features items that are closely related to Slavic culture. Well-known Russian drinks, mysterious characters, weird household items, stylish [and not so] hats and clothes, relatable things in the background.

Do you know why Slavic people hang rugs on the walls? Join our Discord and Twitter to hang out with us on Spaces, AMA's, and the voice chat and find out. We'll educate you on our culture... and teach you how to suka blyat the right way.

Charity

Let's get serious for a second. We truly believe that NFT space can make a difference. It's so heart warming to see more and more projects donate to different causes. Metaverse is cool, but real life doesn't go anywhere.

We're donating at least $30k USD from our mint proceeds to multiple bear conservation foundations. All of the foundations specialize in different species and operate in different regions.

List of our partner organizations:

Pazhetnov family, rescuing and rehabilitating orphaned bear cubs.
The Center rescues, rehabilitates, raises, and prepares orphaned brown bear cubs for independent survival in the wild. Specialists of the Center release rehabilitated cubs into the wild and carry out post-release monitoring to check if the cubs adapt well to their natural habitats.
